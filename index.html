Instructions

The player who first scores 20 or more coins wins.

Hit box: display mystery box item

Collect Coin: their current score will be added to their total coin and then player switch.

  • If the item is coin, add 1 coin to current score.
  • If the item is mushroom, double current coin.
    • If current score is 0: add 2 coins
  • If the item is piranha plant: switch player
